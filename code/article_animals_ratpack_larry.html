Larry

Nobody knows where Larry is from, or when he was born. Everything surrounding his past is a total enigma. It is rumored that his birth certificate is locked up and classified in the Pentagon. When he isn't busy sifting through trash to look for Walmart coupons, he is usually holed up in his lair right next to a run-down pizzeria.
